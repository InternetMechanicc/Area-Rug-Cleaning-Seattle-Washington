Frequently Asked Questions

How often should area rugs be cleaned?

The frequency of cleaning your area rugs varies based on soil levels, with normal soil requiring vacuuming 1-2 times per week and cleaning every 6-12 months, heavy soil necessitating 2-4 times per week vacuuming and cleaning every 3-6 months, and extreme conditions (such as multiple pets or large families) requiring vacuuming 7 times per week and cleaning every 2-3 months.

Is it safe to sleep in room after carpet cleaning?

A general guideline suggests waiting a minimum of 24 hours before sleeping on or being in close proximity to recently cleaned carpets.

How do you clean a large area rug on a hardwood floor?

To clean an area rug on a hardwood floor, begin by vacuuming it on both sides, proceed with shampooing using a specialized rug cleaner, rinse the area rug, and finally, ensure it is thoroughly dried.
